About Me

My name is Suraj Kumar, and I am a dedicated and passionate web developer with a strong foundation in front-end development. I completed my 12th grade at L.P Shahi College and 10th grade at Mother Teresa Tiny Tots English School. I have completed courses in C programming, HTML, CSS, JavScript, PHP, Java, and MySQL, which have equipped me with a solid understanding of programming fundamentals and database management.

I have participated in State and National level hackathons and secured notable rankings, including 4th place in Code 4 Odisha at Siksha 'O' Anushandhan in Bhubaneswar in August 2022 and 5th place in Inventeron at MLRIT HYDERABAD in January 2023. These experiences have honed my team-leading abilities, project management skills, and competitive spirit.

In addition to my events, During my internships at Codsoft, Cognifyz, and IBM, I gained valuable hands-on experience in software development and front-end development. At Cognifyz, I focused on creating responsive and user-friendly web sites and projects, while at IBM, I developed an educational-based gaming website designed to help students learn through interactive games. My project at Codsoft involved contributing to front-end development, enhancing my skills in coding, testing, and debugging.

I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that enhance user experiences and am continuously seeking opportunities to learn and grow in the field of web development. My goal is to leverage my technical skills and creativity to contribute to impactful projects and make a positive difference in the tech industry.
